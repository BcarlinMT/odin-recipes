Zucchini Tacos

A healthy alternative that still tastes great!

Zucchini tacos will have to throwing out other taco shells with their delicious flavor and lots of crunch to go along with it!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups grated zucchini (about 3 small zucchini
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Toppings of choice (recommend scrambled eggs, bacon, cheddar cheese, salsa, avocado, and cilantro)

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 400Â°. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Put zucchini in a strainer and lightly salt. Let sit in the sink or over a bowl to drain as much moisture as possible, about 20 minutes.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together zucchini, almond flour, cheese, egg, and garlic powder. Season with pepper.
  3. Scoop 1/4-cup portions of the mixture onto the baking sheet, then press each gently down until 1/8-inch thick.
  4. Bake until the shells are lightly browned and crisp, about 25 minutes. Let cool slightly, then fill with toppings of choice.
